\part Show the following: \(∀k, l ∈ \Z, k > l : nl = o(n^k)\)

\begin{solution}

Def.: \(o(f) := {g: \N \rightarrow \R_+: \lim\limits_{n \to \infty}\frac{g(n)}{f(n)}=0}\)

Let \(f(n)=n^k\), \(g(n)=n^l\).

\(\lim\limits_{n \to \infty}\frac{g(n)}{f(n)}=\lim\limits_{n \to \infty}n^{l-k}=\lim\limits_{n \to \infty}n^{-(-l+k)}=\lim\limits_{n \to \infty}\frac{1}{n^{k-l}} \rightarrow 0\) since n^{k-l} > 1

\end{solution}
